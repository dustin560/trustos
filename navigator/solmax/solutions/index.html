Three moves that turn signal into shared value.

01 · The Transition Narrative

Articulate why the rebuild.

Center · Communications & Brand · CEO/CFO
A six-week internal exercise: define the strategic narrative behind the 14-month transformation, in language all stakeholders can repeat.
Validates
Clarity. Closes the Intent ↔ Expectation gap for the transition.
Unlocks
A single story the board, investors, customers and employees can tell.
Employees feel oriented through change. Investors get a coherent narrative to back. Customers hear consistency. Trade media gets the story.
02 · Brand Architecture Review

Decide what eight brands do together.

Customers · Employees · Partners
A structured review of the Solmax / GSE / MIRAFI / FABRINET / PROPEX / GEOTUBE / BENTOLINER / MIRAGRID / PETROMAT portfolio — what consolidates, what stays distinct, what gets retired.
Validates
Connection. Does the brand experience cohere across products and regions?
Unlocks
Where to invest brand spend. Which legacy names earn their keep.
Customers experience clarity. Engineers spec faster. Sales talks one story. Distributors carry one banner.
03 · Sustainability Evidence Layer

Convert "we will" into "we have".

Communities · Investors · Regulators
A measurable sustainability evidence pack — Scope 1/2/3, circularity metrics, LCA outcomes per product — published with the Caisse co-investor narrative attached.
Validates
Confidence. Turns sustainability claims into evidence.
Unlocks
Investor letters write themselves. Regulator submissions improve. Sector authority compounds.
The whole ecosystem benefits — communities see real impact, investors see ESG performance, regulators see compliance, customers see the proof point they've been asking for.
