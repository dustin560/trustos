The pattern across every sector

Look at the short edge. It’s nearly always Connection.

In eleven of the twelve sectors we’ve read, Connection is the weakest layer — trailing Clarity by around 18 points. Clarity belongs to comms; Confidence belongs to delivery. Connection is the say-do gap between them that no single function owns — which is exactly what happens when brand has no shared language: the layer that matters most is the layer no one measures. It’s where trust leaks, and the first thing a TrustOS read makes visible.

That gap has a name. It’s the alignment problem — the distance between what an organisation believes and says, and what its stakeholders actually experience. Measuring it is what TrustOS does. Closing it is what MissionCTRL does.
